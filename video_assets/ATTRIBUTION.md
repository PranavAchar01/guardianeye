# GuardianEye promo media attribution

The two promo edits under `out/promo_v2/` use:

- GuardianEye processed demo videos from the project’s public `v0.1.0`
  release.
- “Chelsea fans chanting at Tottenham stadium,” Wikimedia Commons,
  licensed CC BY-SA 2.0. The stadium ambience is taken from this source.
- “Mercedes-Benz Stadium Drone Fly-Over,” Wikimedia Commons, licensed
  CC BY 3.0, as represented in the processed Edge Watch release video.
- UR Fall Detection Dataset footage by Kwolek and Kepski, as represented
  in the processed collapse-detection release video.
- Two original AI-generated GuardianEye establishing images created for
  this edit.
- Original procedural music and sound effects generated by the local edit
  script. No FIFA music, logo, pattern, custom typeface, or other protected
  tournament asset is used.

The motion direction takes broad inspiration from the energy, modularity,
and pacing of modern international-football broadcast packages. It does not
reproduce FIFA World Cup 26 branding.
